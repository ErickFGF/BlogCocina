Chicken Nuggets

Chicken nuggets are one of the quintessential representations of fast food. Those that were born in the 50s as an academic work without a patent and that later settled in the immense fast food chains. Such has been their success that there are many brands that produce and sell them frozen, ready to heat and serve. They are not lacking in children's restaurant menus and they are not only made with chicken, but also with fish or vegetables.

Crab Stuffed Avocados

When two delicious things come together the result is glorious and this is the case with crab stuffed avocados. Two very Ecuadorian products, the avocado from the Sierra, which is used in many dishes, and the crab from the Coast, the famous one for the great crabs, merge here and result in a spectacular dish.
